Mentorship Program

Connect, learn, and grow with guidance from experienced professionals

What is Mentorship?

Our mentorship program connects students and early-career researchers with experienced alumni and industry professionals. It's a structured relationship designed to help mentees develop their skills, gain industry insights, and advance their careers in pharmaceutical sciences.

Whether you're a student seeking guidance or a professional looking to give back, our program provides the framework for meaningful, impactful connections.

Benefits of Mentorship

For Mentees

  • Gain personalized guidance from experienced professionals
  • Develop industry-relevant skills and knowledge
  • Build professional networks and connections
  • Receive feedback on your career path and goals
  • Access insights about job markets and opportunities
  • Build confidence in your career decisions

For Mentors

  • Give back to the community and shape future professionals
  • Refine your leadership and communication skills
  • Stay connected with emerging trends and new perspectives
  • Expand your professional network
  • Contribute to the advancement of pharmaceutical sciences
  • Experience the fulfillment of helping others succeed

How to Get a Mentor (For Students)

Looking for guidance in your pharmaceutical career? Follow these steps to connect with a mentor:

Step 1 Browse Available Mentors

Explore our directory of alumni and researchers who have opted in to the mentorship program. Each mentor has a profile showing their background, expertise, and areas they can help with.

Step 2 Send a Request

Find a mentor who matches your interests and send them a mentorship request. Introduce yourself and explain what you'd like to learn and achieve from the mentorship.

Step 3 Wait for Response

Your potential mentor will review your request and either accept or decline. If accepted, you can start connecting and learning from your mentor!

Step 4 Build Your Relationship

Once matched, engage regularly with your mentor. Come prepared with questions, set goals, and make the most of this valuable opportunity to grow your career.

Become a Mentor (For Alumni & Researchers)

Are you an alumni or researcher ready to share your knowledge and experience? Here's how to become a mentor:

Step 1 Update Your Profile

Make sure your PSRA profile is complete with your professional background, current position, and areas of expertise. This helps students find you based on their interests.

Step 2 Opt-In to Mentorship

Go to your profile settings and enable the mentorship option. This makes you visible in our mentor directory and shows that you're open to receiving requests.

Step 3 Review Requests

When students send you mentorship requests, you'll receive notifications. Take time to review each request and decide if you can help the student achieve their goals.

Step 4 Start Mentoring

Once you accept a request, connect with your mentee. Share your experiences, provide guidance, and help them navigate their career path in pharmaceutical sciences.

Tips for a Successful Mentorship

Be Consistent

Set regular check-ins and stick to them. Consistency builds trust and ensures meaningful progress.

Communicate Openly

Be honest and constructive in feedback. Both mentors and mentees should feel comfortable sharing their thoughts.

Set Goals

Define clear objectives for the mentorship. What does the mentee want to achieve? What can the mentor help with?
