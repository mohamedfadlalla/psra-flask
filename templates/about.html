About PSRA

The Pharmaceutical Studies and Research Association (PSRA) is a dynamic platform connecting pharmacy students and researchers across Sudan. Originally founded at the University of Khartoum, PSRA now serves as a bridge between pharmacy students and researchers from all Sudanese universities.

Our mission is to cultivate a culture of scientific inquiry and innovation among pharmacy students across the nation. We provide opportunities for hands-on research experience, professional development, and community engagement. Through partnerships with industry, academia, and healthcare organizations, we aim to equip our members with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the evolving field of pharmacy.

PSRA is affiliated with the Faculty of Pharmacy Students' Association (FPSA), ensuring alignment with broader student interests and institutional goals. Together, we strive to make a meaningful impact on pharmaceutical education and research in Sudan and beyond.
