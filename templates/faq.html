Frequently Asked Questions

What is PSRA?

The Pharmaceutical Studies and Research Association (PSRA) is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering scientific inquiry and innovation in pharmaceutical sciences. We connect pharmacy students and researchers across Sudan.

Who can join PSRA?

Any pharmacy student, alumni, or researcher affiliated with a Sudanese university can join. Membership is free and open to all who are passionate about pharmaceutical research.

How do I submit my research?

After creating an account and logging in, navigate to the Submit Research page. Fill in the required details about your publication and submit it for admin approval.

How does the mentorship program work?

Alumni and researchers who opt-in as mentors can receive mentorship requests from students. Browse available mentors in the Mentors section and send a request with your goals.

How can I find research projects to work on?

Visit the Projects page to browse open research opportunities. You can filter by skills and apply directly through the platform.

How do I contact support?

Reach out to us through our Contact page or email us at info@psra-sudan.org.
